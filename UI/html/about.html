About TomaLeaf

TomaLeaf AI is a deep-learning web application that detects tomato leaf diseases from a single photo. Upload or paste an image of a tomato leaf and the model returns the most likely condition in seconds — helping growers spot problems early and act before they spread.

How It Works

  1. 1

    Upload a Leaf

    Choose an image from your device or paste one from the clipboard. JPG, JPEG and PNG are supported.

  2. 2

    Feature Extraction

    A pre-trained MobileNetV2 backbone converts the image into a compact feature vector that captures leaf texture, colour and patterns.

  3. 3

    Classification

    A neural-network classifier reads those features and predicts the disease class along with a confidence score.

  4. 4

    Instant Result

    You get the predicted class and a breakdown of probabilities across every disease the model knows.

Conditions We Detect

Bacterial Spot

Small, dark, water-soaked spots caused by Xanthomonas bacteria that can merge and damage leaves and fruit.

Leaf Mold

Pale yellow patches on top of leaves with olive-green to brown mold underneath, common in humid conditions.

Tomato Mosaic Virus

A viral infection causing mottled light and dark green patterns, curling and stunted growth.

Healthy

A clean, vigorous leaf with no signs of disease — exactly what every tomato plant should look like.
